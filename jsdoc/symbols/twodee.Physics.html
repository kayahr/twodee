{twodee.Physics} setDecay(decay)
Sets the decay time in seconds. Default value is 1 second. Decay only makes sense when a life time has been set. Example: Set lifetime to 10 seconds and decay to 2 seconds. The scene node will start fading away (Decreasing the opacity) at 8 seconds and will be invisible and then removed at 10 seconds.
Parameters:
{number} decay
The decay time in seconds
